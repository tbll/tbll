Sakoh Bar: good drinks, but overpriced and overhyped

★★★☆☆
  • Speciality: whiskey
  • Average per drink: 350+
  • Hidden fees (“seating charge” + service charge)

To make your overpriced journey to Ginza Sakoh worth the trip, go with a few friends and order at least 2-3 drinks each to make up for the seating charge. Ginza Sakoh is a nice-looking bar with a decent whiskey collection, but at the moment it’s clearly overpriced, overhyped, and all-in-all, just a bit too grandiose for our liking.

Sidebar: A great bar hiding a gem

★★★★★

Sidebar has an underground feeling. It’s cosy. The drinks are on point. But what makes this place special is that tiny other bar hidden behind the locked gate.

There, you will find a passionate, knowledgable bartender with his glorious gin selection at this bar within a bar.

Definitely worth a visit.
