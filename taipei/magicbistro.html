Magic Bistro. Nothing special really… but the humility of the place, the fact they don't act like super stars, and don't put a useless show… this makes the place special to us. Drinks are amazing. There's some interesting gems hidden among the other bottles. The venue is warm and cosy. Couches. And the subtle light colors (green and red) makes the place a bit hypnotic. It's a little bit hidden. Find the address, get into the tiny garden and claim the stairs.
