review

It’s a little bit hidden. Find the address, get into the tiny garden and go up the stairs.

It’s hard to describe why we love 小城外. Maybe it’s the warmth of the place, the smile of the staff, these on-point cocktail or that feeling of being at home in these comfortable couchs.

Some bars are incredible but everything is ruined by the douchy act of the bartenders and by the huge loud crowd. 小城外 is the oppisite. The staff is humble and very tallented. And I’m pretty sure they don’t even realise how solid and great their cocktails are. The customers are quite is respectful.

It’s just a very comforable place to relax in the evening. And that makes this palce special to us.

Also, there’s some interesting gems hidden among the other bottles, take the time to talk to the bartender!

The venue is warm and cosy. Couches. And the subtle light colors (green and red) makes the place a bit hypnotic.

Words from citynorth:

小城外位於大稻埕ㄧ棟有九十年歷史的老建築內，店內木質調性給人一種沉穩且富有歷史感的氛圍，小城外經典調酒承襲於日本風格，店內環境安靜而恬雅。
