Review

Dadaocheng is famous for its charm of yesteryear, especially those historical buildings from nearly a century ago. In one such building, a homely space on the second floor is cleverly shared by Caffé CityNorth and Bar CityNorth. To enjoy a mellow evening with mind-blowing cocktails, come after 7pm and find Eason, a talented but humble bartender who honed his skills in Japan, ready to show you some real magic. Ask him about those mysterious bottles in the back - you’ll have a good chance of discovering the best of his improvised recipes. With the moody lighting, comfy couches, and the warmth of wood furniture, coming to this unpretentious walk-in only bar is just like dropping by an old friend’s place and being invited to stay longer.
