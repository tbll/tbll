Review

AHA Saloon

Imagine seeing your favourite rock stars performing together on the same stage. It is exactly what happens when you walk into AHA Saloon: three of the few “startenders” from Taiwan in one bar, all extremely knowledgeable and creative…you know a good show is guaranteed for the night! The all-star drinking den is always packed, but if you’re lucky enough to be welcomed in, waste no time and go straight to the bar to meet the award-winning trio. Despite the fame and popularity, they do take time chatting with you, and it’s just like taking a master class of cocktails with your young, friendly professors. The outstanding startenders, professional waiters, beautiful venue and punchy cocktails make AHA Saloon a perfect spot to get your ultimate cocktail experience in the city.
