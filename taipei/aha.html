review

Very new place. Just recently open.

First time I heard about the place, I was told that you won’t be served by regular bartenders, but "Startenders". And indeed: you will meet Victor Yang, award winning bartender, and his colleagues. Very talented and professional. The waiter are very helpful and attentive. Even in rush time, the staff take the time to talk to you and find the right drink for you.

The venue: the place feels like a cosy saloon. Plenty of things all over the walls. Nice couches. Very comfortable. Beautiful.

The cocktails: cocktails are excellent. I believe the bar is called "AHA" because of the feeling of noise we make on the fist sip.

From AHA:

源起於思考過程中的靈光一現 讓調酒師帶領你進入獨特的雞尾酒旅程 點亮腦中的驚喜燈泡，找尋自己的AHA Moment。

Come from an idea flash through people’s thinking process. Let bartenders bring you to our one and only cocktail-journey. Light up the bulb in your brain, and go discover your own AHA MOMENT.
