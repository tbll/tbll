review

Mozi is a weird place.

First, in the tradition of the speakeasy, the place is very much hidden. Get into the hair salon, even if the lights are shut, find the book shelf, push it. And voila, you found one of the best cocktail of Taipei.

Mozi is weird because, the atmosphere is "pub"-like. The crowd is young. People stand, smoke, music can be loud, but at the same time the cocktail and the staff, even with their punk-look, are as professional and talented as you would expect from a classy high standard cocktail bar.

I would recommend to come early and get some food there too.

Do not hesitate to chalenge the bartender. They like it and are very creative. Also, ask them a drink with maqaw pepper. Pretty amazing!

recommendation for dinner:

You can eat at Mozi. There’s also (MUME)[http://www.mume.tw/] close by, but it’s hard to get a reservation. There’s also (Pico Pico)[http://pico-pico-restaurant-bar.business.site/], a south american restaurant. Both highly recommended.
