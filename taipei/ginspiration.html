review

Fun fact: we decided to create this website after visiting Ginspiration. Such a hidden place. Lost on the 3rd floor of a random building in Dadaocheng. On top of a restaurant. Ginspiration is a tinybar.

Perry, the founder of Ginspiration, is the Gin master. He used to sell Gin. And know he makes you taste it. Perry is friendly and humble.

Gin can be complex and subtle. A simple martini won’t make the best Gin justice. Perry, will make you fall in love with this liquor.

If you don’t know Gin, it’s the best place to learn. Perry will teach you. From a simple ricky to a very complex cocktail, Let Perry recommend you a drink. And if you’re already a Gin expert, ask Perry about any obscure Gin he might recommend.

A roasted tomato, few herbs, a complex Gin, et voila, an absolute firework in your mouth. A typical drink there looks like a garden in a glass.

The venue is cute. Small. Intimate.

Also - music is on point (Lofi).

Perry says:

From amateur to profession, first time to be an official bartender. Everything about me in this industry is always linked with gin, so that’s why we call our bar Ginspiration. Gin inspired me and really change my life.
