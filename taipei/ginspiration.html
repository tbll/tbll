Ginspiration 發琴吧

The name says it all. At Ginspiration, gin is the one and only inspiration behind all drinks. Tucked away on the third floor of an old building, the intimate “gin-only” bar may be too small to be noticed, but definitely not too small for you to explore the complexity and subtlety of gin. Former gin seller Perry, who’s also your bartender of the night, knows best how to give his favourite liquor a twist with a slice of roasted tomato or a big cacao shell; you’ll soon find out that a simple martini can’t make the best gin justice. Gin inspired Perry to open Ginspiration; Ginspiration inspired us to create this website. And now, it’s time for you to discover the great potential of gin.
