Mimique

By The Bootleggers List - Taipei 2018. The Top 10 cocktail bars in town.

Mimique is just full of awesomeness. From its luxurious interior, the view from the windows, to its killer cocktails and attentive staff, there is not a single thing that we don’t like about this place! Opposite to its glamorous look with that huge chandelier, Mimique is actually a very inviting bar where you can just relax and enjoy a cosy night whether you’re alone or with friends. While the cocktails, like the venue itself, make perfect Instagram photos, the unique flavour and aroma are what make them truly special. Signature cocktails such as Iron Goddess Tea and Caviar Tonic surprised us with their freshness and depth, simply impressive. The two-storey venue is divided into areas of various styles, including a beautifully furnished smoking area, giving you the chance to explore a different nook every time you return.

Where to eat
