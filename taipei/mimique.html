review

Just opened.

The first thing you’ll notice is the giant chandelier hanging. The 2 floors are built around it.

Great for a date.

Luxurious atmosphere.

You go to Mimique for the atmosphere. So many tiny corners corners. Find the spot you like, and relax.

The staff is very friendly. Do not hesite to go alone and have a chat with the bartender.

Cocktail are great. We recommend the Iron Goddess Tea, very interesting sour and sweet drink, served in a tea cup.

Venue looks beautiful, 2 floors, soon a the 3rd floor (outdoor) will open.

Good for smoker, there’s a dedicated balconny for smoker.

recommendation for dinner:

Very close by, you will find Li’a restaurant. Amazing food!
