Review

We reviewed more that 100 cocktails bars in Taipei. We selected the Top 10 bars in the city that we believe have the best drinks, the best staff, and the best venue.

Mimique is just a place full of awesomeness. From its luxurious interior decoration, the view from the windows, to its killer cocktails and attentive staff, there is not a single thing that we don’t like about this place! Opposite to its glamorous look with that huge chandelier, Mimique is a very cosy and inviting bar; manager Jane is always there to make sure that everybody is in good care. While Mimique’s cocktails, like the venue itself, make perfect Instagram photos, the richness of taste and aroma are what make them truly special. Signature cocktails such as Iron Goddess Tea and Caviar Tonic surprised us with their freshness and depth, simply impressive. Every corner of the space features a different style, giving you a chance to explore a different nook every time you come, including a beautifully furnished area for smokers.
