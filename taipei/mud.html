Mud

By The Bootleggers List - Taipei 2018. The Top 10 cocktail bars in town.

Yes, we do love all those small “hidden gems,” but once in a while, who doesn’t want to have a fun night out with a group of good friends? Mud hears you. This stylish destination is located in the basement of a hotel, well hidden behind an understated entrance and a secretive stairway. Spacious and relaxing, Mud has plenty of designer armchairs and couches for you and your friends to sink in, but the bar itself is also worth a visit. Boasting possibly the best bottle selection in the capital, Mud has brilliant bartenders who know how to make the best out of it, and they love to be challenged. Tell them your preferences and let them surprise you with their talents. We got a shot of rum served “in” a passion fruit, and we absolutely loved it!

Where to eat

There’s a nice Soba noodles restaurant near by called 二月半そば. We also recommend BeApe, a french restaurant.
