review

It’s 4 in the morning, and you’re craving Ramen and a good local single malts? Tender Land got your back. Bar is special as it opens its doors at 11pm and closes at 6am, closed on Friday and Saturday. People say it’s a bar for bartenders. Delicious food. Delicious drinks. The venue is interesting in a way that the kitchen and the staff is surrounded by the bar, and bottles hang from the top.

staff: efficient. venue: dark. interesting layout. opens very late.

recommendation for dinner:

Eat at the bar. Get ramen or beef noodles.
