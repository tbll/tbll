Review

It’s 4am in the morning and you’re craving ramen and some good drinks, where can you go? It’s not easy to find something proper during wee hours in Taipei, but luckily, Tender Land got your back. Open from 11pm to 6am, Monday to Thursday, Tender Land is the secret oasis for peckish night owls and after-work bartenders. (If you come late enough, you might run into bartenders who served you drinks earlier in other bars!) Try to grab one of the 16 seats surrounding the big kitchen/bar, where simple but tasty comfort food like beef noodles and curry rice is prepared and served. For those looking for some quality alcohol, Tender Land’s dazzling whisky collection can never go wrong. Choose from the 100+ bottles hanging on the ceiling and enjoy a tipsy night before the day falls.
