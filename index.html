We are The Bootleggers, a group of anonymous night owls who have visited and reviewed more than 100 cocktail bars in Taipei.

We want to recognize the amazing mixology scene of Taipei.

Our mission is to promote and encourage the many professionals who make the Taipei nightlife such a pleasant experience.

We care about the quality of the drinks, the friendliness of the staff and the comfort of the venue. Our selection includes the most exclusive bars and the most eccentric speakeasy Taipei has to offer.

We are proud to present you The Bootleggers List, the Top 10 Cocktail bars in the city.
